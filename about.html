Why Cclicks?

The world of information will expand infinitely for some time to come. And just like the humans who create these websites, not everyone helps. The webs effective searchability is decreasing as the volume of low-quality information grows.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide a low-tech yet high quality platform. The goal is to retain a timely and relevant collection of information sources available. This will be achieved by dedicating manpower to the "sort and destroy" method. We want our users to have high expectations every time they come back. Why? Because we hold the information directory to high standards too. Go discover anything using our premium directory. Made by the best informationists in the world.
